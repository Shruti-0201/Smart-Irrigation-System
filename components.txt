Smart Irrigation System – Components List

1. ESP32 Dev Board – 1 piece
2. Capacitive Soil Moisture Sensor – 1 piece
3. 1-Channel Relay Module – 1 piece
4. Water Pump (5V or 12V) – 1 piece
5. Jumper Wires – 10 pieces (male-to-male/female)
6. Breadboard – 1 piece
7. Power Adapter or Battery (as per pump rating) – 1 piece
8. USB Cable for ESP32 – 1 piece
9. Smartphone with Blynk App – 1

Optional:
- DHT11 or DHT22 Sensor (for future extension)
- Resistors (for voltage division if needed)
